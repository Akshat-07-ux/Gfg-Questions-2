<h2><a href="https://www.geeksforgeeks.org/problems/count-triplets--141631/1?page=6&category=Linked%20List&sortBy=submissions">Triplets in Sorted Linked List</a></h2><h3>Difficulty Level : Difficulty: Medium</h3><hr><div class="problems_problem_content__Xm_eO" style="--text-color: var(--problem-text-color);"><p><span style="font-size: 18.6667px;">Given the <strong>head </strong>of a linked list sorted in descending order with <strong>distinct </strong>nodes, and an integer<strong> x</strong>, count the number of distinct triplets whose sum is equal to x.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 14pt;"><strong>Examples:</strong></span></p>
<pre><span style="font-size: 14pt;"><strong>Input: </strong>head: 9 -&gt; 8 -&gt; 6 -&gt; 5 -&gt; 4 -&gt; 2 -&gt; 1,  x = 17<br><img src="https://media.geeksforgeeks.org/img-practice/prod/addEditProblem/928052/Web/Other/blobid1_1778926932.webp" width="573" height="81"> <br><strong>Output:</strong> 2
<strong>Explanation:</strong> Distinct triplets are (2, 6, 9) and (4, 5, 8) which have sum equal to x i.e 17.<br></span></pre>
<pre><span style="font-size: 14pt;"><strong>Input: </strong>head: 9 -&gt; 8 -&gt; 6 -&gt; 5 -&gt; 4 -&gt; 2 -&gt; 1, x = 15<br><img src="https://media.geeksforgeeks.org/img-practice/prod/addEditProblem/928052/Web/Other/blobid0_1778926905.webp" width="566" height="80"></span><br><span style="font-size: 14pt;"><strong>Output:</strong> 5
<strong>Explanation:</strong> (1, 5, 9), (1, 6, 8), (2, 4, 9), (2, 5, 8), (4, 5, 6) are the distinct triplets<br></span></pre>
